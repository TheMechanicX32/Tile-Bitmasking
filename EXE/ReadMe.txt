Thanks for downloading my Tile Bitmasking demo from Tuts+

Controls
--------

-Left click to place tiles
-Right click to erase tiles
-Click "Bitmask it!" to perform the Bitmasking calculation on existing placeholder tiles
-Click "Next" or "Previous" to change between static and dynamic examples
